TransferAnything: Enabling Versatile Visual Information Transfer for Creative Image Synthesis

Junjie He, Yifeng Geng, Xuansong Xie
Institute for Intelligent Computing, Alibaba Group
ModelScope Demo

TransferAnything supports the transfer of multiple types of visual information from any region of any image, giving image synthesis a higher degree of freedom and flexibility.

Abstract

In the realm of digital image synthesis, the ability to seamlessly transfer and manipulate multiple aspects of visual information remains a significant challenge. This project introduces TransferAnything, an innovative framework designed to radically enhance image synthesis by enabling the transfer of various types of visual information from any region of a source image to a target image. Our methodology underpins a novel approach that empowers users with unprecedented freedom and flexibility in image composition. Specifically, TransferAnything supports the transfer of layout, color, style, and pixel content, thus facilitating intricate manipulations that were previously complex or unfeasible with traditional techniques. Through a series of experiments and qualitative analyses, we demonstrate the efficacy and versatility of our framework across a wide range of applications, showcasing its ability to generate compelling, high-quality synthesized images. The ongoing development promises to incorporate additional types of visual information transfer, potentially setting a new benchmark in the field of creative image synthesis. TransferAnything not only bridges the gap between professional and novice users by simplifying the image editing process but also opens up new avenues for artistic expression and automated image generation.
